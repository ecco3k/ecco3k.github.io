MOSHERS is a collection of NFTs born from my love of hardcore music, as a way to keep engaged with the scene whilst locked down.

Inspired by DIY hardcore ethics, I will be drawing, coding this website, and creating the smart contract for this project myself with the help of my friends.

MOSHERS 'DEMO' will be limited to 1000, randomly generated, and released as free to mint NFTs on the Ethereum blockchain... For updates on release dates and previews, follow the project on instagram @moshers.io and twitter @moshersnft.
